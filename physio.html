About the Department

The Department of Physiology at Punjab Medical College is dedicated to understanding how the human body works in health and disease. As a cornerstone of the medical curriculum, we bridge the gap between basic sciences and clinical practice.

Our department is equipped with modern laboratories for hematology, respiratory physiology, and cardiovascular studies. We emphasize a research-oriented approach, encouraging students to explore the functional dynamics of physiological systems.
