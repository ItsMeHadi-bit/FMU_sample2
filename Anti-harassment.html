Our Mission

Faisalabad Medical University is committed to providing a safe working and learning environment free from harassment. The Anti-Harassment Cell is constituted to address grievances and ensure strict compliance with the "Protection Against Harassment of Women at the Workplace Act 2010" and HEC guidelines.
